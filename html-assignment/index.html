About Me

Hi, I’m Zak! If I had to give you the CliffsNotes® version of who I am, I’d say that I’m mostly an extrovert; I love music and play any instrument I can get my hands on; I have lots of tattoos and would have my hands tattooed if my mom wasn’t so against the idea; and this is about my 10th year doing some fort of graphic design.

I feel silly to say it but this pandemic has been pretty good for me—other than being in isolation for almost a year, I’ve been able to use the time to figure out what’s really important to me and I’ve even started acting on some of my findings: I’m now in school for design, and I’m about 90% done my first EP (which I’m writing and recording on my own).

"If it doesn't inspire you, you don't need it." -Something I wrote to myself to keep me from buying more guitars
